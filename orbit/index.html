Taper Roller Bearings

Tapered roller bearings consist of inner ring, outer ring, tapered rollers, and cage. The taper angles allow the bearing to handle both radial and thrust loads. The main feature of these bearings is that the action line of the resulting load through the rollers forms an angle with the geometric axis of the bearing, which is why these bearings are especially suitable for supporting significant combined loads.

Truck Hub Bearings

The Truck Hub Unit (Insert Unit) is a fully assembled tapered roller bearing unit along with the sealing and lubrication. This unit is very convenient for a quick and easy replacement in the field. This bearing is maintenance free and has a significantly longer life. It comes with a preset initial tension and the highly efficient sealing system ensures a long and trouble-free service life.

Cylindrical Roller Bearings

The Cylindrical roller bearings consist of inner ring, outer ring, cylindrical rollers, and cage. These bearings are used in applications which have high radial loads combined with high speed. It is easy to fit and dismantle them as they can be taken apart. The bearings are very rigid, thus they can support high radial loads. These bearings are also suitable for higher speeds.

Innovation Strategy

The Orbit Technology Centre focuses on engineering research and development services. It has expertise in areas like metallurgy, material science, lubrication, designing, validation, manufacturing and application engineering. Our engineers work with OEM customers to define application problems and provide most efficient solutions that help end users get the most out of their products. Orbit's adaptability approach has made us integrate new and latest technology in their production cycle. Our capabilities include:


Design and Analysis
Orbit Technology Centre designs bearings as per customer requirements along with added features to enhance product performance and customer satisfaction. We evaluate and refine designs to create superior and longer lasting products


Validation
Orbit has setup in-house testing capabilities and simulation tools for testing our bearings in simulated real road conditions to validate performance and life of our bearings.


Advanced Manufacturing
Our integrated system optimizes manufacturing processes to ensure high quality bearing production. We also offer packaged units that can reduce cost by decreasing vehicle downtime during maintenance and achieve greater life.
